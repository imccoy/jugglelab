Melbourne's community juggling club. A meeting place for all jugglers, unicyclists, hoopers and other circus folk. Everyone is welcome, whether you're a beginner, professional or somewhere in-between. We'll teach you or you can teach us.

When: Wednesdays during school terms, 6:45-8:45pm. If we're not on for some reason, we'll post about it on our facebook page

Where: the National Institute of Circus Arts, 49 Green Street, Prahran

Transport: To get there from the city, catch the train to Prahran or Windsor station on the Sandringham line

Cost: $5 per week

Email: jugglelab@gmail.com
